About Us:

Who are we?

We are the gym for everyone, we have adapted our unique gym to work for you not us from staff that rotate between shifts so there is always someone to assist you and being open 24/7 so if you work late we are still open or if you leave early for work and want to squeeze in a morning workout.

Membership Cards
Because we are open 24/7 we changed how you enter the gym, so now when you enter simply use the card you were given at your introduction on the terminal on the door outside the changing rooms to gain entry however if you do not have a membership card with us and wish to use the gym you can purchase a 24-hour pass that will grant you entry.

Yoga

Yoga is spiritual and physical, it uses a series of breathing, exercise and meditation techniques to improve health.

Boxercise

Boxercise is a high-intensity interval training class based on boxing training. It differs from boxing in that boxing is a competitive sport whereas Boxercise includes aspects of boxing training.

Swimming

Swimming is the use of your limbs to propel yourself through the water.

Zumba

A fusion of Latin international rhythms and easy-to-follow moves. Through both low and high intensity movements, you’ll be dancing your way through this calorie burning dance workout.

Pilates

system of physical conditioning involving low-impact exercises and stretches designed to strengthen muscles of the torso and often performed using specialised equipment.
